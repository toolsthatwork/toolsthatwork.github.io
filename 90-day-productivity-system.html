Phase One: Establishing Your Goals

Begin by defining what success looks like over the next 90 days. Break objectives into monthly milestones to keep momentum. Write each goal down to create clarity and commitment.

Phase Two: Crafting Your Daily Rituals

Select a circadian rhythm that matches your energy peaks. Schedule core tasks during high‑energy windows and reserve low‑energy periods for maintenance. End each day with a quick review to reinforce learning.

Phase Three: Tracking and Adapting

Implement a simple KPI dashboard to visualize progress. Hold a weekly stand‑up to identify bottlenecks and pivot priorities. Celebrate small wins to sustain motivation over the long stretch.
