Time‑Tracking and Calendar Integration

Smart timers blend seamlessly with your calendar, automatically logging active sessions without manual input. They spotlight your most productive hours and flag periods of distraction, giving you a clear picture of where time goes. The resulting data feeds into insightful reports that help you refine your daily routine.

Project Management for One

A lightweight board keeps tasks visually organized and progress visible at a glance. Tagging allows you to prioritize by urgency or seasonality, while automations send you reminders for overdue deadlines. This modest setup replaces bulky spreadsheets and eliminates the overwhelm of scattered to‑do lists.

Communication & Knowledge Wrap‑ups

All messages and client briefs funnel into a single database that auto‑tags content by topic. Sharing these snippets keeps collaborators aligned without endless email chains.
